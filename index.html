Platform Features

Everything in one place.

Macro Tracking

Precision calorie and macro targets — protein, carbs, and fat — calibrated to your body, goal, and activity level.

Intermittent Fasting

Multiple IF protocols including 16:8, 18:6, and custom windows, with fasting timers and meal window reminders built in.

Personalized Workouts

Strength, cardio, and mobility programs designed around your schedule — from 20-minute home workouts to full gym splits.

Progress Analytics

Visual dashboards showing weight trends, strength gains, calorie history, and streak data — so you always know where you stand.

AI Coaching

Smart recommendations that adapt as you progress. Your plan evolves so you never plateau or lose momentum.
